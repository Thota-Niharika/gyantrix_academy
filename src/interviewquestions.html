General Interview Questions

Prepare yourself for interviews with commonly asked general questions by recruiters.

Start with your educational background, key skills, relevant experiences, and end with what you are looking for in your career.

Mention strengths relevant to the job role and a weakness that you are actively improving, showing self-awareness and growth mindset.

Research the company values, culture, and work. Highlight alignment with your goals and enthusiasm for contributing.

Explain techniques you use such as prioritization, time management, and maintaining a positive attitude.

Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to clearly demonstrate your problem-solving skills.
